# Spend_Smart
Spend Smart is a simple budget tracking application designed to help users manage their finances efficiently. With Spend Smart, you can easily track your income, expenses, and savings to achieve your financial goals.

# Features
1. Expense Tracking: Keep track of your daily expenses by categorizing them into different spending categories.
2. Budget Management: Set monthly budgets for various spending categories and monitor your spending to stay within your budget.
3. Income Management: Record your sources of income to get a clear picture of your overall financial situation.
4. Savings Goals: Set savings goals and track your progress towards achieving them over time.
5. User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive and easy-to-use interface makes it simple to navigate and input your financial data.
   
# Getting Started
To get started with Spend Smart, simply visit our website and sign up for an account. Once logged in, you can start adding your income, expenses, and savings goals to track your finances effectively.

# Technologies Used
Spend Smart is built using the following technologies:

1. Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
2. Backend: Node.js, Express.js
3. Database: MongoDB
